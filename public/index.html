Help

Overview

  • Dashboard: View total, original, and duplicate paper counts.
  • All Papers: Lists all papers with duplicate status.
  • Member Pages: View individual member's papers.
  • Charts: Visualize duplicate status and member distribution.

Adding Papers

  • Click "Add Paper" and fill in member, title, link, and optional description.
  • Duplicates are flagged automatically (green for Original, red for Duplicate).

Managing Duplicates

  • Click "Check Duplicates" on All Papers to update statuses.

Data Storage

  • Data is saved automatically to data.json in the app folder.
  • Backup data.json regularly to avoid data loss.
